Increases to your Monthly Charge

The Monthly Charge for your Package (excluding Three Insurance) is the minimum price you agree to pay us for Three Services provided to you under this agreement,
for the Minimum Term. Each April your Monthly Charge will increase by 4.5%. For example:

Monthly price until March 2023 Monthly price from April 2023 to March 2024 Monthly price from April 2024 to March 2025
Price A Price A plus 4.5% (= Price B) Price B plus 4.5% (= Price C)
We've set out an example below, showing how this would work if your Monthly Charge is £30.
£30.00 £30 + £1.35 (4.5% of £30.00) = £31.35 £31.35 + £1.41(4.5% of £31.35) = £32.76

About your allowance - for all Advanced Plan and Mobile Broadband Customers

As part of your Package, you get a monthly allowance of Voice Minutes, Text Messages and/or Data (if you’re on a Mobile Broadband package your plan only includes Data) for use in the UK and subject to paying a daily charge, in our Go Roam destinations.

Using your allowance abroad

There is a £2 daily charge to unlock your UK allowances in Go Roam in Europe destinations and a £5 daily charge to use your UK allowances in Go Roam Around the World destinations. Republic of Ireland and the Isle of Man are excluded from the daily charge. The charge starts when you use any of your allowances in a Go Roam destination including making calls , sending texts or using data and lasts for 24 hours. You can use your plan’s allowance to: (1) call and text back to the UK; (2) call and text between our Go Roam in Europe destinations and; (3) get online - all subject to our fair use policies, which are available in full at www.three.co.uk/priceguide and may be updated from time to time. If you've used all of your plan allowances and are in the EU it's 3p/min to call back to the UK or to another EU country, free to receive calls & texts, 2p/to send an SMS and 1p/MB to use data. Countries not covered in the Go Roam destinations are charged at standard roaming rates at the time of use – www.three.co.uk/priceguide for latest information and pricing.

If you’vechosen a plan with a data allowancegreater than 12GB, a monthly fair use limit of 12GB applies in our Go Roam destinations, with data usage over 12GB (up to your allowance)
being subject to a surcharge.

Other key things to note about your plan

  • If you're calling a number starting 084, 087, 09 and 070 from the UK, Three's access charge is 65p per minute. You'll also be charged a service charge by the company you're calling.
  • All of your data allowance can be used as a personal hotspot within the UK, the EU and the EEA (subject to our Fair Use Policies)
  • Calls to Three Customer Services are free on these plans and won't come out of any allowance.

Additional information about Three Insurance

If added, Three Insurance will protect your Three device. There are two types of cover to choose from. Your chosen cover type is shown above and will be confirmed in your welcome pack, which will be sent to you shortly.

Damage Cover: covers Accidental Damage for your Mobile Phone or Connected Device and provides an extended warranty after the expiry of the manufacturer's warranty.

Full Cover: covers Loss, Theft & Accidental Damage for your Mobile Phone or Connected Device and provides an extended warranty after the expiry of the manufacturer's warranty.

Cover starts at the same time as your new contract. For full terms, including how to complain, go to www.three.co.uk/insurance. Your monthly premium will appear on your Three account (shown on your Three bill under "other charges"). If you complete a claim you'll need to pay a claim excess fee. Your premium is shown in the table above, alongside your choice of Full or Damage Cover and you can find details of the claims excess fees in your Three Insurance welcome pack. If you change your mind you can cancel Three Insurance within 28 days and we'll refund your premium, provided you haven't made a claim. After that you can cancel at any time and cover will finish at the end of the paid period. Your cover will be cancelled if you cancel your Three contract or otherwise after 60 months.
